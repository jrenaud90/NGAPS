About Us

NGAPS was started to help post-docs, contractors, and early career civil servants meet one another and become better acclimated to life at Goddard. NGAPS is run by volunteers across several divisions and labs at Goddard. If you are interested in becoming a committee member, or if you would just like to volunteer at one of our events, please contact one of the officers below.

Contact Us

  • Join the Listserv: If you would like to join the NGAPS listserv (where we advertise all NGAPS happenings) then please email any of the officers below.
  • Events: Do you have a question about an upcoming NGAPS event? Or do you have an event you would like to advertise to the group? Then please e-mail one of the "social" officers below.
  • Other Questions or Concerns: If you have any concerns or questions let us know by using this form!
  • Website Help: If you find an error or have a suggestion for the NGAPS website then please submit it here!
