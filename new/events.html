Regular Events

Due to the ongoing COVID-19 pandemic, all in-person events have been cancelled.

We are looking forward to hosting virtual events. We will continue to update the calendar with those events as they are scheduled. If you need anything during these strange times, please let us know.

Social Lunches

Every Thursday we host an informal lunch at rotating venues across campus. We try to always have an NGAPS+ officer present at these lunches to act as a point of contact. You can find a particular week's point of contact in the event description on the calendar below. We strongly encourage NGAPS+ members to meet up for lunch even when an officer is not available to host.

Time: 12:30pm - 1:30pm

  • 1st Thursday of the Month: Building 34 - Meet at 1st floor Central Stairway or, if the weather is nice, outside near the Cafe.
  • 2nd Thursday: Building 21 - Meet in the cafeteria.
  • 3rd Thursday: Building 33 - Meet at seating area across from cafe.
  • 4th Thursday: Building 1 - Meet at seating area outside gift shop.

LGBTQ+ Members and Allies Lunch

NGAPS+ members are welcome to join the Goddard LGBTQ+ members and allies lunch which occurs on the 2nd Tuesday of each month at 12:30pm. The buildings rotate, please see the description in the calendar below for location details. Also, feel free to contact one of our social officers for more information.
