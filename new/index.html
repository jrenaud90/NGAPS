Welcome to NGAPS!

A place for post-docs and early career scientists at NASA Goddard to network and help each other out

Due to the ongoing COVID-19 pandemic, all in-person events have been cancelled

We are looking forward to hosting virtual events over the coming weeks. We will continue to update the calendar with those events as they are scheduled.

If you need anything during these strange times, please let us know.

The NGAPS mailing list is the best way to stay up-to-day on all NGAPS happenings. To join (even with a non-NASA email), please send an email to one of our officers.
